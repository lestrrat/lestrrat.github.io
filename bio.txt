# English version

*Please feel free to make edits (so as long as the information is accurate), if it is necessary to fit the particular medium*

Daisuke Maki, a.k.a. lestrrat, is the main author of [jwx](https://github.com/lestrrat-go/jwx), [peco](https://github.com/peco/peco), a Go/Perl/C programmer, speaker, author, video producer. In a previous life, he was a serial tech conference organizer (ex-[YAPC::Asia Tokyo](http://yapcasia.org), and ex-[builderscon](https://builderscon.io)) organizer. During his time off from the above, he's a father of two beautiful sons, and works at [Mercari Inc](https://about.mercari.comi/)
